Who We Are

Bharat Samaj LLC is an Indo-American community-focused for-profit organization with a social purpose—dedicated to creating vibrant community centers for Indo-Americans across the USA. Our aim is to establish Bharat Community Centers across the USA as a one-stop destination for cultural celebration, professional networking, and community growth. Rooted in our rich Indian heritage, we aim to provide a religion-neutral space where culture thrives, and individuals connect and grow.

At Bharat Samaj, we believe in building bridges that unite every corner of the Indo-American community. Our mission is to establish Bharat Community Centers across the USA, creating spaces where families, cultures, and industries come together to celebrate traditions, foster connections, and drive growth.

These centers will include spaces for families to interact, host cultural events, and provide resources that cross regional, linguistic, and industry boundaries.

Key Highlights

  • Religion-neutral premises (with a temple as part of the center).
  • Open to all Indians—North, South, East, or West.
  • A growing organization aiming to unite and empower Indo-Americans.
  • A one-stop hub for Indian cultural events (of national presence to begin with) and professional networking.
  • Shares allocated to legal members of the LLC, with future plans to grow into an Inc.
